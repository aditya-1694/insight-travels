About Us

Insight Travels and Tours is a family-owned travel agency established in 1994 and certified by IATA. For over three decades we have supported global clients with reliable, efficient and highly responsive travel solutions.

Our team works closely with corporations, doctors, insurance providers and international partners to manage complex travel requirements worldwide.

Our Services

Medical Evacuation & Repatriation

Global coordination of emergency medical evacuations and repatriation services. We work closely with doctors, hospitals and insurance providers to ensure safe and timely transport of patients worldwide.

Corporate Travel

End-to-end travel management for businesses including airline ticketing, hotel reservations, airport transfers, visas and full logistics coordination.

Events & Conferences

Complete travel and logistics management for conferences, corporate offsites and international events.
